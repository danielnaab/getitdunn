Thank you to our sponsors!

Platinum Gold Silver Bronze Friends Kids' Fun Run

This event is possible because of the generous support of our sponsors. All event proceeds are directed to scholarships, camperships and needs-based gifts awarded by Mind Over Matter (M.O.M.), Inc., the nonprofit presenter of the Get It Dunn run.

The Get It Dunn event is a great family event that has drawn nearly 6,500 participants over the past 10 years. Racers have traveled to Menomonie, WI from 25 different states plus US Virgin Islands, Canada & Germany. Becoming a sponsor is a great way to showcase your organization's support for fitness and and healthy communities.

If you’d like to become a sponsor, we offer several different sponsorship packages. We also accept in-kind donations of race materials for sponsorships. See below for sponsorship levels or view/download the sponsorship one-sheet.

We’d also like to thank the Dunn County Sheriff’s Office, Menomonie Police Department and the Dunn County ARES/RACES Team (Amateur Radio Emergency Service/ Radio Amateur Civil Emergency Service) for their ongoing support of this event. Along with our volunteers, these groups ensure the safety of our race courses.

Get It Dunn Event Sponsorship Levels

To become a sponsor please email Karen, Nate or Tom to discuss how your organization can be involved. Commitment to sponsorship level before March 15, 2024 is required to ensure sponsors receive full sponsor recognition as set forth below. Upon receipt of payment/written acknowledgment of sponsorship, sponsorship will be acknowledged and marketed as noted.

PLATINUM SPONSOR: $5,000+

  • Organization name/logo on finish line arch
  • getitdunnrun.com homepage ad
  • Logo on all marketing materials & event shirt
  • Social media posts highlighting organization’s sponsorship
  • Announcer recognition at start, finish and post-race celebration
  • Logo on a mile marker
  • Donate items for runner bags
  • Logo and link on getitdunnrun.com
  • Ten entries for race
  • Recognition in PSAs & television spots (when available)
  • Signage at post-race celebration (provided by sponsor)
  • Booth at post-race celebration

GOLD SPONSOR: $3,500

  • Organization name/logo on post-race celebration arch
  • getitdunnrun.com homepage ad
  • Logo on race posters & event shirt
  • Logo on event shirt
  • Water stop sponsorship
  • Announcer recognition at start, finish and post-race celebration
  • Logo on a mile marker
  • Donate items for runner bags
  • Logo and link on getitdunnrun.com
  • Six entries for race
  • Signage at post-race celebration (provided by sponsor)
  • Booth at post-race celebration

SILVER SPONSOR: $2,000
