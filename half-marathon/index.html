1/2 Marathon

Race Results

7:45 a.m. Start — Half Marathon

The FAST AND FLAT half marathon starts at 7:45 a.m. near 9th Av. East and 6th St East, one block south of Burger King. Runners will journey through Menomonie’s east side, through the Dunn County Recreation park, around the Stout Technology park and then begin heading north onto Menomonie’s beautiful trail system which will take them by Menomin Park and onto Menomonie’s newest trail that crosses utilizes a bike/walk bridge crosses over Lake Menomin and then the trail continues to follow the lake as you head into Wakanda Park. As the trail temporarily ends in Wakanda Park, the course reconnects with the trail system that will take the runners south back towards downtown, again overlooking Lake Menomin.

Top finishers will be awarded prizes as will the largest team.

Don't forget about after the race-the post race celebration will be rocking to celebrate your accomplishment!

Also if you bring your bib to Log Jam after the race you will get a free drink!

Registration & Fees

There is a non-refundable and non-transferable entry fee of:

  • $50 per person if you register before 01/30/23
  • $55 per person if you register between 01/31/2023 and 03/30/2023
  • $65 per person if you register between 03/31/2023 and 05/14/2023
  • $70 per person if you register between 05/15/2023 and race day

* 05/1/2023 is cut off date for shirt size designation.

Race entry includes an official Get It Dunn Run race t-shirt! 1/2 marathon finishers will receive a custom-designed GIDR 13.1 medal!
