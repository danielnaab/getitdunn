Schedule

FRIDAY

3:00 — 7:00 p.m. Race Packet Pick-up and Registration

Burger King located at 544 Main Street East, Menomonie, WI 54751
• Race packet pick-up (individuals can pick up race packets for friends/family)
• Race registration (cash or check only )

SATURDAY — RACE DAY!

6:30 – 8:00 a.m. Race registration & packet pick-up

Burger King located at 544 Main Street East, Menomonie, WI 54751
• Race registration (cash or check only)
• Pick up your race packet if you didn’t on Friday. You’ll want your shirt and timing chip!

PARKING

City parking is available throughout downtown Menomonie. Our event takes place around the University of Wisconsin — Stout campus, We have access to the following UW parking lots, free of charge:

• Lot 1 located on Wilson Ave (about 1 ½ blocks SW of Burger King)
• Lot 24 located on 9th Ave (just east of the Home Ec building) is about one block south of Burger King
• Lot 10 (on 9th Ave) is near Lot 1
UW-Stout Parking Lot Map Guide


7:45 a.m. Start — Half Marathon

The NEW in 2017, FAST AND MUCH FLATTER half marathon starts at 7:45 a.m. near 9th Av. East and 6th St East, one block south of Burger King. Runners will journey through Menomonie’s east side, through the Dunn County Recreation park, around the Stout Technology park and then begin heading north onto Menomonie’s beautiful trail system which will take them by Menomin Park and onto Menomonie’s newest trail that crosses utlizes a bike/walk bridge crosses over Lake Menomin and then the trail continues to follow the lake as you head into Wakanda Park. As the trail temporarily ends in Wakanda Park, the course reconnects with the trail system that will take the runners south back towards downtown, again overlooking Lake Menomin.

8:30 a.m. Start — 5K

The 5K starts one block east of Wilson Park, on 6th Street, right in front of Stout lot 24. Participants are encouraged to congregate in the lot before the race starts. Parking is available around the area. Keep in mind the post-race celebration at the Burger King parking lot, one block east from the finish line on Wilson Avenue.

9:00 a.m. Post-Race Celebration

Burger King parking lot, located at 544 Main Street, Menomonie, WI 54751
• Live music, food & refreshments for all race participants
• Get your finisher photo taken — individuals and teams!
• Awards presentation for top finishers & teams
• T-shirts & other race items for sale

After-Race Bar Crawl!

Use your race bib after the race to redeem one free drink (21 years or older) at the Waterfront Bar & Grill (opening early on race day-10:00 a.m.). Also redeem a free domestic beer or a rail drink with your race bib at the Log Jam (breakfast served until 10 a.m.). If you want to continue the celebration, participants can get a free drink at the The Arena and The Market starting at 11:00a.m. by again showing your race bib. Establishments are located within a couple blocks of the Post-Race Celebration at Burger King.
