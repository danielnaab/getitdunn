GET IT DUNN 5K + 1/2 Marathon

Presented by Mind Over Matter (M.O.M.) Inc. and the Brooke Richartz Foundation Inc.

Time to GET IT DUNN again! ➜ 6.10.2023 ➜ Menomonie, WI

Covid may have detoured our plans for the last few years, but we are BACK. Join us June 10, 2023 for the Get It Dunn Run 5K and half marathon — and NEW in 2023, Brooke's kids' fun run! Although many things will remain as they were, we are making a few changes to make the event even better than in the past. Stay tuned to more information and registration opening.  

We introduced a NEW, fast and much flatter 1/2 marathon course in 2017 which received WORLD wide recognition after the Guinness Book of World Records was shattered, and then shattered again in 2018. Then in 2019, a different World Record was set! ( USATF Certification #WI17005DM )

Why GET IT DUNN?

★ Awesome t-shirts for all participants and finisher medals for ALL 1/2 marathon finishers
★ Prizes for top finishers and largest team

Pushing through that last mile is worth more than you thought...

Your participation not only benefits your wellness, it also directly helps the local community through Mind Over Matter (M.O.M.) scholarships, camperships and needs-based giving! ALL event proceeds fund M.O.M. outreach — thank you!

♥ M.O.M. gave 285 children over $56,000 in summer camperships in 2015-2022
♥ M.O.M. awarded $15,000 in national scholarships to 14 students 2014-2022
♥ In the last fourteen years M.O.M. gave up to $3,000 annually to Kathleen Fisher Memorial Scholarship winners
♥ In our 9 years of the Get It Dunn Run we have had over 4,000 registered participants from 23 different U.S. States, Plus Canada, Mexico, Germany and the U.S. Virgin Islands.

All proceeds raised from the Get It Dunn Run go to Mind Over Matter (M.O.M.) Inc. and the Brooke Richartz Foundation, Inc., both non-profit organizations that donates thousands of dollars to local youth for camperships, scholarships and provide professional mentoring to assist youth in developing a roadmap for their future professional careers.
