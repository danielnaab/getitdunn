GET IT DUNN 5K + Kids Fun Run!

Presented by Mind Over Matter (M.O.M.) Inc. and the Brooke Richartz Foundation Inc.

Time to GET IT DUNN again! ➜ 6.8.2024 ➜ Menomonie, WI

After returning post the Covid-hiatus, 2023 saw record numbers and times. Join us June 8, 2024 for the Get It Dunn Run 5K and Brooke's Kids' Fun Run! There will NOT be a half marathon in 2024.

Why GET IT DUNN?

★ Awesome t-shirts for all 5k participants who register by 5/1/24.
★ Prizes for top finishers and largest team

Pushing through that last mile is worth more than you thought...

Your participation not only benefits your wellness, it also directly helps the local community through Mind Over Matter (M.O.M.) scholarships, camperships and needs-based giving! ALL event proceeds fund M.O.M. outreach — thank you!

♥ M.O.M. gave 300 children over $65,000 in summer camperships in 2015-2023
♥ M.O.M. awarded $17,000 in national scholarships to 15 students 2014-2023
♥ In the last fifteen years M.O.M. gave up to $3,000 annually to Kathleen Fisher Memorial Scholarship winners
♥ In our 10 years of the Get It Dunn Run we have had over 4,500 registered participants from 25 different U.S. States, Plus Canada, Mexico, Germany and the U.S. Virgin Islands.

All proceeds raised from the Get It Dunn Run go to Mind Over Matter (M.O.M.) Inc. and the Brooke Richartz Foundation, Inc., both non-profit organizations that donates thousands of dollars to local youth for camperships, scholarships and provide professional mentoring to assist youth in developing a roadmap for their future professional careers.
