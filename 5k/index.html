5K

Race Results

The 5K begins at any time you want! You have between May 1 and May 17, 2020 to complete your virtual race.  When you complete your event, please share your pics on Facebook AND email your results to Raceberry (a link will be provided).  Top finishers will be awarded prizes as will the largest team.  All participants acknowledge that they are responsible for their own safety and assume all risk.  In addition, participants must adhere to all COVID-19 regulations in place. 

Shirt pick up locations will be posted as the date nears.  If you want a shirt mailed or tax receipt, you must contact Tom at tom@getitdunnrun.com and make the request.

Top finishers will be awarded prizes as will the largest team.

See a full letter about our COVID-19 response from our race director Here.

Registration & Fees

There is a non-refundable and non-transferable entry fee of:

  • $25 per person if you register before 12/31/2019
  • $30 per person if you register between 01/01/2020 and 02/29/2020
  • $35 per person if you register between 03/01/2020 and 04/11/2020
  • $40 per person if you register between 04/12/2020 and 05/12/2020

* 04/12/2020 is cut off date for shirt size designation.

Race entry includes an official Get It Dunn Run wicking t-shirt!
