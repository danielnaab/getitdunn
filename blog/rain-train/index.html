Rain train

Terry Sullivan / May 6, 2014

We’ve had a tough winter with plenty of rain. While we hope for a perfect race day, we never know. That’s why, next time it rains, put on the gear you plan to wear race day and get outside. It is better to find out what gears works best on training day rather than race day. Plus, it’s actually pretty fun to run in the rain!

Hope training is going well!
